Cap One - an Airbnb Helper

Inspired by the Capital One Mindtap Challenge and made for CS50

Made with Sara Kvaska

Cap One helps users maximize profits from renting out their homes by applying a unique algorithm to public neighborhood data from Airbnb. On its interactive map, users can find existing neighborhood listing data, an optimal price point, and an estimated weekly revenue for a given location at that price point. They can also save listing history if they have more than one property-- this framework serves as the beginning of an investment portal. In the floating side bar, users can see pricing and review trends by neighborhood, visualized in easy to understand graphs. In a ruthless and gentrified San Fransisco housing market, we aim to help make life a little more livable for both tenants and landlords.

Although our site is no longer live, as it was run via flask on an IDE, you can check out our walkthrough video by clicking on any one of the screenshots below.
